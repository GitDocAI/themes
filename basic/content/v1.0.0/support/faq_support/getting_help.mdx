# Getting Help

This page provides information on how to get support for UTMStack, report issues, and access various community and learning resources.

## Official Support Channels

For direct assistance and support, UTMStack offers several official channels:

*   **Support Portal**: Access the dedicated support portal at [https://support.utmstack.com](https://support.utmstack.com).
*   **Email Support**: Send an email with your questions to [support@utmstack.com](mailto:support@utmstack.com).
*   **Website Contact Request**: Initiate a contact request directly from the official UTMStack website at [https://utmstack.com](https://utmstack.com).

## Accessing the Online Demo

To experience UTMStack, you can access the online demo.

*   **Online Demo**: Visit [https://utmstack.com/demo](https://utmstack.com/demo).

### Troubleshooting Demo Access

If you encounter issues loading the demo site, consider the following troubleshooting steps:

1.  **Wait**: Allow approximately 10 seconds for content or a login form to appear.
2.  **Refresh**: Refresh the page to attempt reloading the login form or interactive content.
3.  **Search for Known Issues**: Search Google for "UTMStack demo site not loading" to find workarounds or status updates.
4.  **Check Official Website**: Open [https://utmstack.com](https://utmstack.com) in a new tab to look for official updates or alternative demo access options.
5.  **Use "Online Demo" Button**: Click the 'Online Demo' button on [https://utmstack.com](https://utmstack.com) to attempt accessing a working demo environment.
6.  **Request Human Control**: If further progress requires manual intervention (e.g., email-based credential retrieval) or clarification, human control may be necessary.
